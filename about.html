About Us

MUHAMMAD YAWAR Welcome to the WebP Image Converter! This simple tool was created by Muhammad Yawar to make converting images into the WebP format quick and easy.

Our goal is to help users reduce image file sizes while maintaining quality, which is especially helpful for web performance and storage savings.

All conversions happen locally in your browser — we do not upload or store your images. It's private, secure, and super fast.

Whether you're a developer, designer, or just someone with a bunch of JPGs or PNGs to convert, we hope this tool makes your work easier.

Thanks for using our converter!
